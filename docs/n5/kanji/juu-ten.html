Context & Study Examples

明日は十月十日で私の親友の誕生日なので、みんなで集まってサプライズパーティーを開いて盛大にお祝いをしようと計画しています。

Ashita wa juugatsu tooka de watashi no shinyuu no tanjoubi na node, minna de atsumatte sapuraizu paatii o hiraite seidai ni oiwai o shiyou to keikaku shite imasu.

Tomorrow is October 10th and it is my best friend's birthday, so we are planning to gather together, throw a surprise party, and celebrate grandly.

この本を読み終えるまでにはまだ十時間くらいかかると思いますが、内容が非常に興味深いので最後まで一気に読んでしまいたい気分です。

Kono hon o yomioreiru made ni wa mada juujikan kurai kakaru to omoimasu ga, naiyou ga hijou ni kyoumibukai node saigo made ikki ni yonde shimaitai kibun desu.

I think it will take about ten more hours to finish reading this book, but since the content is so interesting, I feel like reading it all at once until the end.

私が子供の頃、この町にはまだ信号機が十個もありませんでしたが、今では発展して大きなビルやショッピングモールがたくさん建っています。

Watashi ga kodomo no koro, kono machi ni wa mada shingouki ga jukko mo arimasen deshita ga, ima dewa hatten shite ookina biru ya shoppingu mooru ga takusan tatte imasu.

When I was a child, there weren't even ten traffic lights in this town, but now it has developed and many large buildings and shopping malls have been built.
