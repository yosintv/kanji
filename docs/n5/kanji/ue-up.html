Context & Study Examples

テーブルの上に置いておいたはずの車の鍵がどこにも見当たらないので、家族の誰かが間違えて自分のカバンに入れてしまったのかもしれません。

Teeburu no ue ni oite oita hazu no kuruma no kagi ga doko ni mo miataranai node, kazoku no dareka ga machigaete jibun no kaban ni irete shimatta no kamo shiremasen.

The car keys that were supposed to be on the table are nowhere to be found, so maybe one of my family members accidentally put them in their own bag.

日本語の会話が早く上手になりたいのであれば、毎日少なくとも三十分間は声に出して教科書を読む練習を欠かさず続けることが何よりも大切です。

Nihongo no kaiwa ga hayaku jouzu ni naritai no de areba, mainichi sukunakutomo sanjuppunkan wa koe ni dashite kyoukasho o yomu renshuu o kakasazu tsuzukeru koto ga naniyori mo taisetsu desu.

If you want to become skillful at Japanese conversation quickly, it is more important than anything to consistently practice reading the textbook aloud for at least thirty minutes every day.

デパートの屋上に上がると街の景色を一望することができて、特に天気が良い日には遠くにある山々まで綺麗に見えるのでおすすめです。

Depaato no okujou ni agaru to machi no keshiki o ichibou suru koto ga dekite, tokuni tenki ga yoi hi ni wa tooku ni aru yamayama made kirei ni mieru node osusume desu.

When you go up to the department store rooftop, you can get a panoramic view of the city; I recommend it especially on clear days because you can see the distant mountains beautifully.
