年

Year

来年の夏休みには、今まで一生懸命に貯めてきたお金を使って家族と一緒に一ヶ月間かけてヨーロッパをゆっくりと旅行する予定を立てています。

Rainen no natsuyasumi ni wa, ima made isshoukenmei ni tamete kita okane o tsukatte kazoku to issho ni ikkagetsukan kakete yooroppa o yukkuri to ryokou suru yotei o tatete imasu.

For next year's summer vacation, I am making plans to travel leisurely through Europe with my family for a whole month using the money I have worked hard to save up until now.

私が日本に来てから今年でちょうど三年になりますが、最初は全く分からなかった日本語も今では日常生活で困らない程度に話せるようになりました。

Watashi ga nihon ni kite kara kotoshi de choudo sannen ni narimasu ga, saisho wa mattaku wakaranakatta nihongo mo ima dewa nichijou seikatsu de komaranai teido ni hanaseru you ni narimashita.

It has been exactly three years this year since I came to Japan, and I am now able to speak Japanese well enough to not have trouble in daily life, even though I didn't understand it at all at first.

一年の計は元旦にありという有名な言葉があるように、新しい年が始まった最初の日にその年の一年間の目標をしっかりと決めることは非常に大切です。

Ichinen no kei wa gantan ni ari to iu yuumei na kotoba ga aru you ni, atarashii toshi ga hajimatta saisho no hi ni sono toshi no ichinenkan no mokuhyou o shikkari to kimeru koto wa hijou ni taisetsu desu.

As the famous saying 'the plan for the year is made on New Year's Day' goes, it is extremely important to firmly decide on your goals for the year on the very first day the new year begins.
