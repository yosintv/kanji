九

Nine

九月に入ると少しずつ涼しい風が吹き始めて、夏の厳しい暑さが和らぎ、公園の木々も次第に秋の色に変わり始めます。

Kugatsu ni hairu to sukoshizutsu suzushii kaze ga fukihajimete, natsu no kibishii atsusa ga yawaragi, kouen no kigi mo shidai ni aki no iro ni kawarihajimemasu.

As we enter September, a cool breeze starts to blow bit by bit, the harsh summer heat eases, and the trees in the park gradually begin to change into autumn colors.

明日の朝は九時からの会議に遅れないように、いつもより一時間早く家を出て、駅前のカフェで資料の最終チェックをするつもりです。

Ashita no asa wa kuji kara no kaigi ni okurenai you ni, itsumo yori ichijikan hayaku ie o dete, ekimae no kafe de shiryou no saishuu chekku o suru tsumori desu.

I intend to leave the house an hour earlier than usual tomorrow morning to not be late for the 9:00 meeting and do a final check of the materials at the cafe in front of the station.

九つのリンゴを家族みんなで分けるのは少し難しいので、半分に切ってから全員が同じ量だけ食べられるように工夫することにしました。

Kokonotsu no ringo o kazoku minna de wakeru no wa sukoshi muzukashii node, hanbun ni kitte kara zen'in ga onaji ryou dake taberareru you ni kufuu suru koto ni shimashita.

Dividing nine apples among the whole family is a bit difficult, so we decided to cut them in half and find a way so that everyone can eat the same amount.
