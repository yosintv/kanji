一月の一日は日本で一番大切な祝日なので、多くの人々が家族と一緒に新しい年のお祝いをして過ごします。

Ichigatsu no tsuitachi wa nihon de ichiban taisetsu na shukujitsu na node, ooku no hitobito ga kazoku to issho ni atarashii toshi no oiwai o shite sugoshimasu.

January 1st is the most important holiday in Japan, so many people spend time with their families celebrating the new year.

一日中ずっと家で勉強していたので、気分転換のために少しだけ外へ出て散歩をしてこようと思います。

Ichinichijuu zutto ie de benkyou shite ita node, kibun tenkan no tame ni sukoshi dake soto e dete sanpo o shite koyou to omoimasu.

Since I have been studying at home all day long, I think I'll go outside for a short walk to change my mood.

まずは一冊の教科書を最後までしっかり読み終えてから、次の新しい教材を買うかどうかを決めるつもりです。

Mazu wa issatsu no kyoukasho o saigo made shikkari yomiorete kara, tsugi no atarashii kyouzai o kau ka dou ka o kimeru tsumori desu.

I intend to finish reading one textbook thoroughly to the end before deciding whether or not to buy the next new teaching material.
