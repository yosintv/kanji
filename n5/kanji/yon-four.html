四

Four

私の家族は父と母と弟、そして私の四人家族で、週末はいつもみんなで一緒に美味しい夕食を食べるのが楽しみです。

Watashi no kazoku wa chichi to haha to otouto, soshite watashi no yonin kazoku de, shuumatsu wa itsumo minna de issho ni oishii yuushoku o taberu no ga tanoshimi desu.

My family consists of four people: my father, mother, younger brother, and myself, and I look forward to us all eating a delicious dinner together on weekends.

大学の近くにあるアパートは四階建ての古い建物ですが、家賃がとても安いので学生たちには非常に人気があります。

Daigaku no chikaku ni aru apaato wa yonkaidate no furui tatemono desu ga, yachin ga totemo yasui node gakuseitachi ni wa hijou ni ninki ga arimasu.

The apartment near the university is an old four-story building, but it is very popular among students because the rent is very cheap.

四月になると桜の花が満開になり、日本の公園は花見を楽しむたくさんの人々でどこも賑やかになります。

Shigatsu ni naru to sakura no hana ga mankai ni nari, nihon no kouen wa hanami o tanoshimu takusan no hitobito de doko mo nigiyaka ni narimasu.

When April comes, the cherry blossoms reach full bloom, and Japanese parks everywhere become lively with many people enjoying flower viewing.
