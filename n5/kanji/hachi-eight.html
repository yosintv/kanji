八

Eight

hachi

Onyomi Reading
ハチ
Kunyomi Reading
や、や-つ、や-っつ、よう

Grammar & Contextual Usage

八月は夏休みの真っ最中なので、海や山へ遊びに行く計画を立てている子供たちが多く、街全体がとても賑やかな雰囲気になります。

Hachigatsu wa natsuyasumi no massaichuu na node, umi ya yama e asobi ni iku keikaku o tatete iru kodomotachi ga ooku, machi zentai ga totemo nigiyaka na fun'iki ni narimasu.

August is right in the middle of summer vacation, so there are many children making plans to go to the sea or mountains, and the entire town takes on a very lively atmosphere.

テーブルの上に並んでいる八つの美味しいドーナツの中から、どれを最初に食べるか迷ってしまいますが、やはりチョコが一番気になります。

Teeburu no ue ni narande iru yattsu no oishii doonatsu no naka kara, dore o saisho ni taberu ka mayotte shimaimasu ga, yahari choko ga ichiban ki ni narimasu.

I'm having trouble deciding which one to eat first from the eight delicious donuts lined up on the table, but the chocolate one definitely catches my eye the most.

毎朝八時になると近くの小学校のチャイムが聞こえてくるので、それを合図に私も急いで仕事に行くための準備を終わらせるようにしています。

Maiasa hachiji ni naru to chikaku no shougakkou no chaimu ga kikoete kuru node, sore o aizu ni watashi mo isoide shigoto ni iku tame no junbi o owaraseru you ni shite imasu.

Every morning at 8:00, I can hear the chime of the nearby elementary school, so I use that as a signal to finish my preparations for going to work in a hurry.
