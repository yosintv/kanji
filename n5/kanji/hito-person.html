あの有名なレストランの前には、美味しい料理を食べるために毎日たくさんの人が長い列を作って一時間以上も待っています。

Ano yuumei na resutoran no mae ni wa, oishii ryouri o taberu tame ni mainichi takusan no hito ga nagai retsu o tsukutte ichijikan ijou mo matte imasu.

In front of that famous restaurant, many people form a long line every day and wait for over an hour just to eat delicious food.

日本人は礼儀正しくて親切な人が多いという印象を持っていましたが、実際に日本に来てみてその通りだと改めて実感しました。

Nihonjin wa reigitadaushikute shinsetsu na hito ga ooi to iu inshou o motte imashita ga, jissai ni nihon ni kite mite sono toori da to aratamete jikkan shimashita.

I had the impression that Japanese people are often polite and kind, and having actually come to Japan, I felt once again that it is indeed true.

一人で静かに過ごす時間も大切ですが、週末に大好きな友達と一緒に笑いながら過ごす時間は、私にとって何よりも貴重なものです。

Hitori de shizuka ni sugosu jikan mo taisetsu desu ga, shuumatsu ni daisuki na tomodachi to issho ni warainagara sugosu jikan wa, watashi ni totte naniyori mo kichou na mono desu.

Time spent quietly alone is important, but time spent laughing with dear friends on the weekend is more precious to me than anything else.
