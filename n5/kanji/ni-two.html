Grammar & Contextual Usage

来週の月曜日と火曜日の二日間、出張のために大阪へ行かなければならないので、今からホテルの予約をします。

Raishuu no getsuyoubi to kayoubi no futsukakan, shucchou no tame ni Oosaka e ikanakereba naranai node, ima kara hoteru no yoyaku o shimasu.

Since I have to go to Osaka for a business trip for two days, next Monday and Tuesday, I will make a hotel reservation now.

私の目の前には二つの道がありますが、どちらが正しい方向なのか分からないので地図をもう一度確認してみます。

Watashi no me no mae ni wa futatsu no michi ga arimasu ga, dochira ga tadashii houkou na no ka wakaranai node chizu o mou ichido kakunin shite mimasu.

There are two paths in front of me, but I don't know which one is the correct direction, so I will check the map once more.

仲の良い友達と二人で静かなカフェに行って、将来の夢や最近あった出来事についてゆっくりと語り合いました。

Naka no yoi tomodachi to futari de shizuka na kafe ni itte, shourai no yume ya saikin atta dekigoto ni tsuite yukkuri to katariaimashita.

I went to a quiet cafe with a close friend, and the two of us talked leisurely about our future dreams and recent events.
